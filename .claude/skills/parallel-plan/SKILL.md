Whenever analysis, design or implementation is required, do the following: 1) define the problem and success criteria, if needed read `skills/design-grill/SKILL.md` and follow its process to establish a common understanding. 2) Fanout five sub-agents or agent teams, each takes a different approach to solving the problem. 3) Synthesize the results of all sub-agents or agent teams in a report and propose a plan to the user.
